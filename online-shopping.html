üõ° How to Stay Safe While Shopping Online

  • Research online stores before buying. Check reviews and website age.
  • Only pay with secure methods like credit cards or PayPal, not wire transfers or crypto for regular purchases.
  • Be cautious of deals far below normal prices.
  • Look for HTTPS (the secure lock) on websites.
  • Save all receipts, messages, and proof of purchase.
